Thank you for installing the AI Translate Chrome extension!

Translate any text on a webpage effortlessly with AI.

How to Use

Step 1: Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle icon in your Chrome toolbar, then click the pin next to AI Translate.

Step 2: Highlight Text & Right-Click

Highlight any text and right-click to see the AI Translate logo in the context menu. Access your last 8 copied items from the popup.

Make sure Chrome is running in the background.

Step 3: Click the Extension Logo

Click it to see a small popup with translation info for the highlighted text.

Optional: Change Target Language

The extension automatically detects your browser language. To change it, click the extension logo, select your desired language, and confirm.
